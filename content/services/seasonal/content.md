## Residential & Commercial Snow Clearing

To protect your concrete, asphalt, and surrounding landscaping, we intentionally do not use large plows or heavy machinery. All of our snow removal is executed using high-capacity snowblowers and manual shoveling. This approach allows for detailed, precise clearing around vehicles, walkways, and sensitive property lines without the risk of heavy mechanical damage.

### Snow Removal & Ice Management
We provide reliable clearing for driveways, laneways, and commercial pedestrian areas. Following the physical removal of snow, we provide strategic salt and ice-melt applications as needed to prevent hazardous ice buildup and ensure safe traction.
